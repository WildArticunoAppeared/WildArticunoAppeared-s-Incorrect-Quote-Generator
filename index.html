Yet another incorrect quote generator! Now with retroactively changeable names and pronouns!

This incorrect quote generator has been created with the ability to change the names and pronouns in mind. Have you ever got a quote where a different set of characters than the ones you put in would've been more suitable? Or did you put the characters in in the wrong order? Well, with this one, all you have to do is change the names and pronouns and then click Update Names! The quote will remain the same, but the names and pronouns will be changed.

DISCLAIMER: Unfortunately, however, this generator doesn't have any shipping or NSFW filters yet. I may add one in the future, which would include filters for more than just sexual quotes, but I've pulled out enough hair for now trying to get the retroactive name/pronoun change feature to work. So... sorry if you get any unwanted NSFW or shipping quotes.

The quotes included in this generator are from memes, Tumblr, Twitter, other incorrect quote generators, YouTubers Luke Correia, Jeany Collects and Nyctomatter and YouTuber Admiral Adorable's OMORI meme dubs.
